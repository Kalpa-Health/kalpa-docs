# Weekly Update Ritual — Tuesday Intro

**For:** Team meeting, week of 10 March 2026
**Purpose:** Introduce the Friday async update habit. 5 minutes, not a ceremony.

---

## What to say

We are going to do one short written update every Friday. Not a status report — just a foothold so I know where everyone is before Tuesday without having to ask in chat. It takes 5 minutes to write and saves us 20 minutes of catch-up in this meeting.

The format is in `kalpa-docs/meetings/weekly-update-template.md`. Three sections: done, pending, blocking. Fill it in, commit to `kalpa-docs`, open a PR. I will read it over the weekend or Monday morning and comment if anything needs discussion Tuesday.

**Two rules:**
1. Done means merged — not "almost done." If it is not merged, it goes in pending.
2. Blocking means you cannot proceed without something from someone else. Name the person or decision. Do not leave it as a vague note.

That is it. I am not grading the length. Short is fine.

---

You have full autonomy on implementation. Two things require a conversation with me first:

1. Changing or making an exception to an ADR
2. Missing the 2-week go-live window

Everything else is your call. Rename my variables. Rip out my code. I do not care.

## Expected outcome

By end of Friday each week: one PR per person in `kalpa-docs` with their update filled into the template. Alex reads async. Tuesday meeting starts with decisions and blockers, not status.
